# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

### **What is 2d6 Dice Roller?**

2d6 Dice Roller is a free app for rolling virtual dice. You can roll standard dice types: D2, D4, D6, D8, D10, D12, D20, and D100. You can also save your favorite combinations as Dice Sets for quick access.

### **Is the app free?**

Yes, 2d6 Dice Roller is completely free with no in-app purchases.

### **Do I need to create an account?**

No. There is no account, login, or sign-up required. Just open the app and roll.

---

## Dice Sets

### **What are Dice Sets?**
Dice Sets let you save your favorite dice combinations so you can jump straight into any game. When creating a set, you can also select a specific game from the list. 2d6 will save the game context alongside your dice so the correct custom faces load automatically every time. For example, you could save a Zombie Dice set, a D&D combat set, or a custom Yahtzee setup.

### **How do I create a Dice Set?**
Tap the Dice Sets icon in the toolbar, then tap + to create a new set. Choose a game from the picker, select your dice types and quantities, give it a name, and save.

### **How do I load a Dice Set?**
Tap any set from the Dice Sets list. 2d6 will clear the table and load your saved dice with the correct skins instantly.

### **Can I edit or delete a Dice Set?**
Yes. Swipe left on any set to show the edit and delete actions. Tapping edit will bring up the form so you can update the name, game, or dice amounts.

### **Are my Dice Sets backed up?**
Dice Sets are stored locally on your device. They are not synced to the cloud or any external server.

## Games & Die Skins

### **What are die skins?**
Die skins replace the standard numeric faces with custom faces for specific games. For example, Zombie Dice uses Brains, Footprints, and Shotgun Blasts, while King of Tokyo uses Claws, Energy, and Hearts.

### **How do I switch games?**
Use the side menu to select a game. 2d6 will clear the table and automatically load the correct dice and skins for that game. No manual setup needed.

### **What happens to my dice when I switch games?**
The current dice on the table are cleared when you switch. Make sure to save a Dice Set first if you want to come back to your current configuration.

### **Which games are supported?**
The following games are currently supported with custom dice and skins:

1. Catan: Cities & Knights - loads one custom d6 with faces for 3 City Gates (Yellow, Blue, Green), and 3 Barbaian Ships.
1. Zombie Dice — draws 3 random dice from a weighted pool of 6 green, 4 yellow, and 3 red dice
2. King of Tokyo — loads 6 custom d6s with faces for points (1, 2, 3), Energy, Attack, and Heal
3. King of New York — loads 6 custom d6s with faces for Energy, Attack, Heal, Destruction, Celebrity, and Ouch!
4. Poker Dice — loads 5 d6s with card rank faces: 9, 10, Jack, Queen, King, and Ace
5. Kingdom Death: Monster — loads 2 d10s with a lantern symbol in place of the 10 face
Any Game — loads 2d6 and lets you build freely with any dice type

### **Can I add more dice after switching games?**
Yes. After switching, the Add Die option will only show dice available for the current game, so you always roll with the right faces.

## Rolling

### **How do I roll the dice?**
Tap anywhere on the scene to roll. The dice will tumble and settle, so you can see the results. For convenience, you can also choose to have the results displayed on the screen. From the "..." button, select Results, and then choose the option you want.

### **Can I roll different types of dice together?**
Yes. You can mix and match any combination of die types in a single roll.
